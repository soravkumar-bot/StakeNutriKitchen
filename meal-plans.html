Weekly Meal Plans

Three structured meal plan tracks to get you started — adjust portions, swap meals, and adapt to your schedule. These are starting templates, not medical prescriptions.

⚠️ Calorie estimates are approximate. Individual needs vary based on age, weight, activity level, and health status. Consult a healthcare provider or registered dietitian for personalized advice.

🥗 Balanced Diet Plan

Designed for general wellness, sustainable energy, and healthy eating habits. Approximately 1,600–1,900 kcal/day with balanced macronutrients.
